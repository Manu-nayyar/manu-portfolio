Multilingual SEO for a Plastic Manufacturer Targeting Regional India

Built a production-grade WordPress site from the ground up, then implemented multilingual SEO, local SEO, and programmatic product pages to capture high-intent searches across regional Indian markets.

The Challenge

Free India Plastic Works is a plastic manufacturing business serving customers across multiple Indian states. Their challenge was visibility: manufacturing buyers in India search in regional languages, use location-specific queries, and heavily rely on Google Maps. A standard English-only site simply wouldn't capture this audience.

Additionally, the business needed a scalable way to show up for hundreds of specific product searches — different plastic product types, materials, and specifications — without manually creating individual pages.

  • No existing web presence — built entirely from scratch
  • Target audience searches in Hindi, Tamil, Kannada, and English
  • Critical need for Google Maps / local pack visibility
  • 200+ product SKUs requiring individual SEO-optimised pages
  • Competitors with established domain authority in the space

The Approach

Foundation

Site Architecture & Technical Build

  • Built the full website on WordPress from scratch — custom theme, performance-optimised
  • Designed URL structure and site hierarchy to support multilingual SEO from day one
  • Implemented hreflang tags correctly across all language/region variants
  • Page speed optimisation: images served as WebP, lazy loading, minimal CSS/JS, CDN
  • Core Web Vitals tuning to pass Google's LCP, CLS, and INP thresholds
Local SEO

Google Business Profile & Map Pack Strategy

  • Full Google Business Profile setup and optimisation
  • Citation building across Indian business directories (Justdial, Indiamart, Sulekha, etc.)
  • Location-specific landing pages for each major city/region served
  • Consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) signals across all platforms
  • Review acquisition strategy for social proof and ranking signals
Programmatic SEO

Product Page Generation at Scale

  • Built a template-based programmatic page system for all product categories
  • Auto-generated SEO-optimised pages for product × location combinations
  • Implemented Product schema markup across all programmatic pages
  • Crawl budget optimisation — proper robots.txt, XML sitemap structure, canonical tags
  • Internal linking architecture to pass authority from high-traffic pages to product pages
The multilingual architecture wasn't just about translation — it required understanding how search intent differs across languages and regions, and structuring the site so Google understood which pages to show to which users.

Technical Highlights

  • hreflang implementation for 4 language variants with correct x-default handling
  • Sitemap architecture separating multilingual, local, and programmatic pages
  • Schema markup: LocalBusiness, Product, BreadcrumbList, FAQPage
  • Mobile-first design with sub-3s load time on 4G connections

Key Learnings

Manufacturing SEO in India is fundamentally about understanding that buyers don't all search in English. Capturing regional-language queries requires real architectural decisions — not just Google Translate.

  • hreflang must be implemented at the server level for reliability — plugin-based solutions often create errors at scale
  • Local SEO for manufacturing is heavily driven by citation consistency — one incorrect NAP variation can suppress rankings
  • Programmatic pages need careful quality signals — thin pages without differentiation get filtered by Google
  • Map pack rankings drove more phone enquiries than organic website traffic — proving local SEO ROI clearly
